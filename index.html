Simple Budget

Hello, my name is Icram Doku. I am the creator and author of this website. I found myself struggling with money and did some research. I found this 50/30/20 Rule and started following it. It worked greatly for me, so I thought I should share it.


As a high school student (with a job) and now a college student, I still struggle to save enough money. I hate asking my parent for money at the end of the month. So, with some research, I found this simple rule and found it very easy to follow!

Of course, this rule applies when you are a beginner (like me) to saving. If you are saving for a big purchase like a new car or a house, this will only help you to get in the habit of saving. You will gradually have to increase the percent of your saving!

With the disclaimers out of the way, have fun! Play around with it. Budget better for future and prospect income.
